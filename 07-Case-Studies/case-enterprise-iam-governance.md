# Case Study: Enterprise Identity Governance Program

## Client Profile
Mid-size enterprise organization with hybrid infrastructure.

## Challenge
Fragmented identity management created compliance and insider risk exposure.

## Engagement Scope
- Identity lifecycle automation
- Privileged access review
- Policy alignment
- Audit evidence generation

## Solution
- Implemented automated offboarding
- Integrated CAASM visibility
- Established governance workflows
- Created executive reporting

## Technologies
Axonius, Python, PowerShell, Active Directory, NIST 800-53

## Results
- Reduced orphaned accounts
- Improved audit readiness
- Strengthened access controls
- Increased operational efficiency

## Business Impact
Enabled leadership to manage identity risk proactively.
