# Identity Offboarding Automation

## Source Project
https://github.com/dlgul71/identity-offboarding-automation-engine

## Business Problem
Manual offboarding created orphaned accounts and compliance risks.

## Solution
- Automated account disablement
- Removed access across systems
- Generated audit logs
- Enforced joiner/mover/leaver controls

## Technologies
Python, PowerShell, Active Directory, Cloud IAM

## Results
- Reduced orphaned accounts
- Improved compliance posture
- Lowered insider threat risk
- Increased operational efficiency

## Business Value
Strengthened identity governance and reduced regulatory exposure.
