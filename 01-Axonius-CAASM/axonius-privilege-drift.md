# Axonius Privilege Drift Detection

## Source Project
https://github.com/dlgul71/axonius-privilege-drift-detector

## Business Problem
Organizations lacked visibility into privilege escalation and access drift,
creating audit and insider risk exposure.

## Solution
- Integrated Axonius asset and identity data
- Analyzed privilege changes over time
- Automated alerts and reporting
- Supported governance and compliance reviews

## Technologies
Axonius API, Python, IAM Data, NIST Concepts

## Results
- Improved privileged access visibility
- Reduced excessive permissions
- Strengthened audit readiness
- Enhanced IAM governance

## Business Value
Enabled leadership to proactively manage access risk and regulatory exposure.
